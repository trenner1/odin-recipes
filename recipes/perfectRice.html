Perfect Rice

Description:

Cooking rice is the most basic kitchen task in Asian cooking, yet many people have trouble creating fluffy white rice that is not mushy.

However, once you learn the tricks, youâ€™ll find that cooking perfect rice is the simplest thing to do!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup long grain white rice
  • 1 3/4 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon salted butter

Steps:

  1. Rinse the rice with tap water, gently wash with hands, and discard the water. Repeat 3 times. Cover rice with water again and let rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Transfer rice to a sieve and drain well.
  3. Transfer the rice to a medium dutch oven (or heavy pot with a tight fitting lid) and add water to cover.
  4. Cover and cook over medium heat. As soon as the water is boiling, turn to low heat. Cover and simmer for 12 minutes. Uncover and take a quick peek at the rice. If there is water left, cover and let cook for another 1 to 2 minutes.
  5. Remove the the pot from the stove. Let rest, covered, for 10 to 20 minutes.
  6. Fluff the rice with a rice paddle and serve warm.

Notes:

  • You can use half the amount of water and substitute the other half for chicken broth for added flavour.
  • You can have 'good enough' rice by skipping the rinsing and draining
  • A great cast iron mini dutch oven makes this process very easy
